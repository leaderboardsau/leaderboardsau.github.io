A remastered version of Grand Theft Auto 3, Grand Theft Auto: Vice City and Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas is coming to PS4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox Series S, Xbox Series X and Nintendo Switch in the coming weeks.

Amazon Australia has just posted the product page for the game in Australia, and it’s the cheapest price we’ve seen.

Grand Theft Auto: The Trilogy – The Definitive Edition will be the first GTA game on Nintendo Switch. Currently only the physical PS4 version of the game is available for pre-order on the Sony side of things. No direct gameplay footage for the game has been shown yet, meaning the Nintendo Switch version of the game hasn’t yet been shown.

According to Rockstar Games you can “play the genre-defining classics of the original Grand Theft Auto Trilogy: Grand Theft Auto III, Grand Theft Auto: Vice City, and Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas updated for a new generation, now with across-the-board enhancements including brilliant new lighting and environmental upgrades, with high-resolution textures, increased draw distances, Grand Theft Auto V-style controls and targeting, and much more, bringing these beloved worlds to life with all new levels of detail.”

The game also includes a GTAV-style controller layout Improved gunplay and targeting controls, with upgraded drive-by controls in GTA: San Andreas. As well there are updated Weapon and Radio Station selection wheels, updated Mini-Maps with enhanced navigation allowing players to set waypoints to destinations, the ability to immediately restart a failed mission, plus new Rockstar Games Social Club Accomplishments for members.

Amazon’s lowest price guarantee means if you pre-order the game today you be charged the lowest price at launch, if the price does decrease. EB Games also has trade offers. The digital versions of the game release November 11.
